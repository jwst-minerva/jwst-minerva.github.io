MINERVA publications to date

A community treasury program.

MINERVA builds on a long legacy of public extragalactic imaging surveys in the UDS, EGS, COSMOS, and GOODS-N fields: HST/CANDELS, JWST/PRIMER, JWST/CEERS, JWST/JADES, and many more. We thank the many astronomers who contributed to the design, execution, and analysis of these surveys. MINERVA has no proprietary period, and we aim to release high-quality public data products (PSF-matched images, photometric catalogs, stellar population catalogs, morphological catalogs) on rapid turnaround. We welcome community use of our data products.
