NIRCam, MIRI, and HST image mosaics

The reduced NIRCam mosaics from the MINERVA UDS-2 observations are now available! NIRCam and HST data is reduced consistently using GRIZLI and drizzled to a 0.04" pixel scale. MIRI mosaics are reduced using a custom version of the pipeline used for the SMILES survey and drizzled to 0.08" pixel scale. Broad-band and F410M NIRCam mosaics as well as the MIRI F770W and F1800W mosaics were acquired by the PRIMER team (PI: Dunlop). The mosaics below also include re-reductions of HST data from the CANDELS survey (Grogin et al. 2011, Koekemoer et al. 2011) to ensure rest-frame optical coverage for low-redshift objects. Additional details will be included in a future data release publication.

Future Data Releases

As a large treasury program, our team is committed to providing high-quality data products for the full community. Please expect PSF-matched imaging, photometric catalogs, stellar population synthesis catalogs, and morphological catalogs for all existing JWST and HST fields. Data products will be released within 6-12 months of data acquisition; our scheduling windows are listed below and described in detail in our survey paper.

UDS-2: July 18 - Aug 10, 2025 (data acquired)

COSMOS-2: Nov 18 - Jan 7, 2026 (data acquired)

UDS-1: Jan 4 - Jan 25, 2026

COSMOS-1: April 16 - April 27, 2026

AEGIS: June 16 - June 27, 2026

GOODS-N: Jan 31 - Feb 10, 2027
