Data Releases

Coming soon.

As a large treasury program, our team is committed to providing high-quality data products for the full community. Please expect PSF-matched imaging, photometric catalogs, stellar population synthesis catalogs, and morphological catalogs for all existing JWST and HST fields. Data products will be released within 6-12 months of data acquisition; our scheduling windows are listed below and described in detail in our survey paper.

UDS-2: July 18 - Aug 10, 2025

COSMOS-2: Nov 18 - Jan 7, 2026

UDS-1: Jan 4 - Jan 25, 2026

COSMOS-1: April 16 - April 27, 2026

AEGIS: June 16 - June 27, 2026

GOODS-N: Jan 31 - Feb 10, 2027
